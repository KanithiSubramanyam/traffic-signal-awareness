Traffic Signals Awareness

Card title

Some quick example text to build on the card title and make up the bulk of the card's content.

Do's For Red Signal

Stop Completely: Bring your vehicle to a full stop at the stop line or before the crosswalk.

Observe Traffic Laws: Follow all local traffic laws and regulations regarding red lights.

Check for Pedestrians and Other Vehicles: Look for pedestrians and other vehicles that may be crossing.

Follow Right on Red Rules In some places, you may turn right on red after stopping and ensuring the way is clear.

Use Turn Signals: Signal your intentions if you plan to turn once the light changes. If it's right use right signal or left use left signal.

Don'ts For Red Signal

Do Not Run the Red Light: Never drive through a red light; it’s illegal and dangerous.

Avoid Distractions: Don’t use your phone or engage in other distractions while waiting.

Do Not Block Crosswalks: Ensure your vehicle does not block crosswalks, allowing pedestrians to cross safely.

Don't Make Illegal Turns: Don't make turns or other maneuvers that are not allowed by traffic signs or signals.

Don’t Inch Forward: Avoid inching forward into the intersection while waiting for the light to change.
